HELP

Assuming that either the tst or igra tests have been performed, this app allows the clinician to go through a number of questions to arrive at the probabilty that the test has true PPV. It also allows the clinician to examine the NICE guidelines regarding the diagnosis and treament of TB.

FIRST SCREEN

Using the left hand menu, choose New Patient. Go through the questions in order. And field marking with "*" must be completed. In certain circumstances (such as if the patient is from India), more questions will appear. These are not obligatory fields, but will assisit in producing a more accurate assessment of the tst. If information is inconsistent or missing, and warning message is given.

Click the right hand arrow to progress to the next screen

SECOND SCREEN

The second screen is a list of medical conditions which might have a bearing on the test reading. Select all which apply, or leave blank if none apply.

Click the right hand arrow to progress to the next screen, or go back to make any corrections

RESULTS

This presents the probabilty that the test(s) have a positive Predictive value. Full details of the calculation performed can be found in the "Documentation" section in the left-hand menu. This screen also presents a simple summary of relevant NICE information. More detailed information can be found in the NICE section of the left-hand menu.
